Overview

BingeList is a social media app that lets users instantly review and recommend TV shows to their peers. The app focuses on quick, digestible content that helps users decide what to watch next based on peer reviews and trending lists. My goal with BingeList is to create a platform that reduces decision fatigue, and bring about a more engaging way to discover new shows to watch.
