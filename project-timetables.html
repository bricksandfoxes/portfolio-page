Overview

Tabletables is a mobile app which helps students at Mizzou and other universties view bus and other transit schedules in a clean, easy-to-read format. Given the current issues with the usability of the GoCOMO app, I wanted to create a solution that would make it easier for students to navigate transit options around campus and the city.
